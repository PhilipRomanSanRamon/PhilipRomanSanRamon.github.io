Zion-Benton Township High School "Tech Crew"

IT apprenticeship | August 2015 - May 2019

TechCrew is a 4-year student-led program in which students provide tech support and maintenance of the school's computers and other equipment. Working with the school's IT support staff, students can expect to learn the ins and outs of servicing computer hardware and software, customer service/support, leadership, and much more. Summer work and scholarships are available to qualifying students. TechCrew is open to all district students who want to and are willing to learn. Interested students can find applications in the Tech Office during the opening weeks of each semester.

  • Customer service

  • Server maintainence
